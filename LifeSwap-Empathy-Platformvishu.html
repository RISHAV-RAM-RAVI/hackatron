How LifeSwap Works

Our AI-powered platform creates immersive experiences that help you understand different perspectives and build empathy.

1

Choose a Life

Browse through our diverse collection of real-life stories from people around the world. Select a perspective that intrigues you or challenges your assumptions.

2

Live Their Story

Immerse yourself in their daily routine, challenges, and triumphs through interactive storytelling. Make decisions as they would and experience the consequences.

3

Reflect and Share

After your experience, reflect on what you've learned and how it's changed your perspective. Share your insights with our community and see how others responded.
